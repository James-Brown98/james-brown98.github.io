Scope

JavaScript scope refers to where variables are initiated and can be acessed. Variables can be seen to have a loval or a global scope. For global scope this would mean that the variable would have to be intiated outside of a function and this will mean that it can be accessed both within and outside of a function, as long as it is below where the variable was set. Local scope means that when you initiate a variable inside of a function, then it can only actually be accessed within that function and won't necassarily exist outside of that function, therefore cannot be accessed/changed outside of the function.
